Terms of Service
Last updated: May 2026 · Errands Logistics Solutions

By using Errands, you agree to these Terms. Please read them carefully. These Terms govern your use of the Errands platform, including posting errands, running errands, and using Errands Escrow.

1. Who We Are

Errands is a trust-first errand marketplace operated by Errands Logistics Solutions, registered in Nigeria. We connect users who need tasks done with vetted runners who execute those tasks. We also operate Errands Escrow, a secure payment holding service.

2. The Errands Platform

Errands is a marketplace platform. We do not employ runners. Runners are independent contractors who offer their services through our platform. Errands facilitates the connection, verifies identity, and provides the trust infrastructure — but the service agreement is between the user and the runner.

We reserve the right to remove any user or runner from the platform for behaviour that violates these Terms or undermines the trust and safety of the community.

3. User Responsibilities

As a user you agree to:

• Provide accurate information when creating your account

• Post only lawful errands — no illegal activities, no hazardous materials

• Pay for completed errands promptly and fairly

• Treat runners with respect and dignity

• Not attempt to contact or transact with runners outside the Errands platform to circumvent our service fee

4. Runner Responsibilities

• Complete accepted errands to the best of your ability and as described

• Communicate promptly and honestly with users through the platform

• Never misappropriate funds, items, or information entrusted to you

• Maintain your verified status — notify Errands of any changes to your identity or circumstances

• Not solicit users to transact outside the platform

5. Payments and Fees

Errands charges a service fee on each completed errand. The current fee structure is displayed at the time of payment. Fees are subject to change with notice.

For Errands Escrow, a service fee is charged based on the tier selected. Fees are deducted at the time of escrow creation and are non-refundable unless the escrow is cancelled before funding.

6. Cancellations and Refunds

User cancellation before runner accepts: Full refund to wallet.

User cancellation after runner accepts: 50% of the agreed fee is payable to the runner for time and inconvenience. Remainder refunded to wallet.

Runner cancellation: No fee charged to user. Full refund. Runner's reliability rating is affected.

Disputed errands: Handled according to our Dispute Resolution Policy.

7. Liability

Errands acts as a facilitator. We are not liable for the actions of runners or users, loss of items, damage to property, or failure to complete an errand except where directly caused by a platform failure. We strongly recommend using Errands Escrow for any transaction involving payment for goods.

8. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be resolved in Nigerian courts.

Questions about these Terms? Contact us at legal@errands.ng

Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026 · Compliant with NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation)

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019.

1. What We Collect

Account information: Name, email, phone number, city, password (encrypted)

Runner verification: NIN, government-issued ID, selfie — stored encrypted, used only for identity verification

Transaction data: Errands posted, payments made, escrow transactions

Communications: In-app messages between users and runners

Usage data: How you use the app, which features you access

2. Why We Collect It

• To verify your identity and maintain platform trust and safety

• To facilitate errands and process payments

• To resolve disputes and enforce our Terms of Service

• To improve our platform and personalise your experience

• To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. How We Protect It

All data is stored on encrypted servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Identity documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. In-app messages are private and not accessible to third parties.

We use Supabase — a SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure — for all data storage.

4. Who We Share It With

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

• Payment processors (Paystack) — to process transactions

• Identity verification partners — to verify runner identities

• Law enforcement — when required by Nigerian law

5. Your Rights (NDPR)

Under the NDPR you have the right to:

• Access the personal data we hold about you

• Request correction of inaccurate data

• Request deletion of your account and associated data

• Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

Privacy questions? Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@errands.ng

Dispute Resolution
How we resolve disputes
We take every dispute seriously. Our goal is a fair outcome for both user and runner within 48 hours.
How to raise a dispute

Tap the Dispute button on any active errand or escrow transaction. Describe the issue clearly. Attach any evidence (photos, screenshots) if available.

All disputes must be raised within 48 hours of the errand completion or escrow release date.

Resolution timeline
0-4 hrs

Dispute acknowledged. Errands team notified. Both parties contacted for information.

4-24 hrs

Evidence reviewed. Errands team makes a preliminary assessment.

24-48 hrs

Final decision communicated to both parties. Funds released or refunded accordingly.

Possible outcomes

Full refund to user — runner failed to complete the errand or misappropriated funds/items

Full payment to runner — errand was completed as agreed and user dispute is unfounded

Partial settlement — errand was partially completed, payment split accordingly

Escalation — for complex disputes involving significant sums, we may involve a neutral third party

Contact our disputes team directly at disputes@errands.ng or call +234 800 ERRANDS
